Books & Writing

Becoming Light.

Becoming Light is Tsilah Burman's memoir — a story of transformation, healing, and the journey toward inner freedom. Through her writing, Tsilah explores what it means to move through darkness, listen for the soul's wisdom, and become more fully alive.

Her writing is rooted in honesty, embodiment, spiritual inquiry, deep inner emotional work, and the tender process of remembering the light within. It is for readers who are walking their own path of becoming, or seeking to begin that path — and looking for inspiration, hope, and a reminder of the guidance and resilience we all carry within us.

Songs for the journey home.

Music is a healing balm, reminding us of where we've been and who we are. Music inspires us to keep going and to become our best selves. Tsilah has written songs that relate to the themes of Becoming Light — companions to the book, and to the journey it tells.
