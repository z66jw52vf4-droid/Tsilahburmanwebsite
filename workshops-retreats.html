Workshops & Retreats

Welcome Home to Your Body.

Welcome Home to Your Body workshops and retreats are sacred, supportive spaces for women to gather, reflect, move, listen, and reconnect with themselves. These experiences are created for women who long to feel more at home in their bodies, more connected to their truth, and more free to live from the inside out.

Through guided reflection, embodied practices, gentle movement, writing, sharing, and ritual, participants are invited to release old ways of holding and open to a more compassionate, peaceful, and luminous relationship with themselves.

What to Expect

Leave with more than you came with.

  • Reconnect with the body as home.
  • Explore what peace and freedom feel like from within.
  • Listen to the wisdom beneath old stories.
  • Share in a circle of support and presence.
  • Leave with practices for continued integration.
