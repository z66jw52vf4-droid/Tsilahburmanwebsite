Welcome Home to Your Body

Come home to the wisdom within.

Coaching, movement, workshops, and writing for women who are ready to find greater peace, freedom, and a deeper connection with themselves.

Begin Your Journey Explore the Work

There is a place within you that has always known the way. A place beneath the striving, the pleasing, the holding, and the forgetting. My work is an invitation to return to that place — through the body, through breath, through honest reflection, through movement, through story, and through the quiet courage of becoming more fully yourself.

I'm Tsilah Burman, author of Becoming Light and creator of Welcome Home to Your Body. I guide women into deeper self-connection, helping them listen to the wisdom of their bodies, soften old patterns, and discover a more peaceful and liberated relationship with themselves.

About Tsilah

The body is not something to overcome. It is a doorway.

Tsilah Burman is an author, coach, movement teacher, and workshop facilitator devoted to helping women come home to themselves. Her work weaves together embodied awareness, compassionate inquiry, movement, story, and spiritual listening.

Through Welcome Home to Your Body, Tsilah creates spaces where women can breathe more deeply, feel more honestly, and reconnect with the light within them.
